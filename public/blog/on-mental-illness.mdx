>"If sanity and insanity exist, how shall we know them?"
> > David Rosenhan, "On being sane in insane places" (1973)

<br />

In his landmark 1973 study, David Rosenhan recruited eight different volunteers
and posited them in various psychiatric institutions, posing as patients who
recently reported hearing sounds of "thud," "empty," and "hollow." Each of his
patients received a diagnosis of schizophrenia and were quickly sorted into
each institution.
